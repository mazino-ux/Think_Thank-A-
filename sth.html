An Introduction

Who We Are

The Think-tank Training Academy is a high-end executive business school established in Nigeria.

We specialize in IT, strategic management, and leadership. We offer professional and executive short courses, as well as diplomas in IT, management courses, health and social care management, tourism and hospitality management, and strategic management and leadership.

At Think tank, our main goal is to create a community of leaders in various industries who can improve the global economy.

We focus on partnerships to improve our students' performances. We do this by preparing them for their careers, offering online, classroom learning and blended learning, using competency-based education, and providing real-life case studies.

Furthermore, we give unlimited academic support to our students, to make learning easier. Students get to learn from industry experts who bring their professional experience into the classroom.
